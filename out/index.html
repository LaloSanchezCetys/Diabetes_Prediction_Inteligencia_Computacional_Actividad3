About This Model

This dataset is originally from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases. The objective is to diagnostically predict whether a patient has diabetes based on certain diagnostic measurements.

Dataset Constraints:

  • All patients are females
  • Minimum age: 21 years old
  • All patients are of Pima Indian heritage
Input Ranges
Input fields are restricted to specific ranges based on the dataset's characteristics. Please ensure all values are within the acceptable ranges shown below.

Number of pregnancies (0-17)

Glucose level in blood (0-199 mg/dL)

Blood pressure measurement (0-122 mmHg)

Thickness of skin (0-99 mm)

Insulin level in blood (0-846 mIU/L)

Body mass index (0-67.1)

Diabetes percentage (0.08-2.42)

Age in years (21-81)
